Seal of the Living God

God the Father has given His children the Seal of the Living God for protection in the end times. Accept this Seal and recite this Crusade Prayer with faith.

Crusade Prayer (33) — Seal of the Living God O my God, my loving Father,
I accept with love and gratitude
Your Divine Seal of Protection.
Your Divinity encompasses my body and soul for eternity.

I bow in humble thanksgiving
and offer my profound love and loyalty
to You, my Beloved Father.

I beg You to protect me and my loved ones
with this special Seal and
I pledge my life to Your service forever and ever.

I love You, Dear Father.
I console You in these times, Dear Father.
I offer You the Body, Blood, Soul and Divinity
of Your dearly beloved Son
in atonement for the sins of the world
and for the salvation of all Your children.

Amen.

The Eucharistic Marian Remnant Army

The Eucharistic Marian Remnant Army is a spiritual movement dedicated to the defense of the Holy Eucharist, devotion to the Blessed Virgin Mary, and the proclamation of the prophetic messages given to the world in these end times. We are soldiers of Christ, formed under the mantle of Our Lady, armed with the Rosary, the Scapular, and the Seal of the Living God.

In these times of great apostasy and tribulation, God the Father, Jesus Christ, and the Blessed Mother have spoken through chosen messengers to warn humanity, call souls to repentance, and prepare the faithful Remnant Church for the glorious era of peace — the Eucharistic Reign of the Sacred Heart and the triumph of the Immaculate Heart of Mary.

We stand firm in the Traditional teachings of the One, Holy, Catholic, and Apostolic Church. We reject the spirit of compromise and modernism that has infiltrated the sanctuary. We uphold the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass, Holy Communion received on the tongue, and the fullness of Catholic doctrine as handed down by the Apostles and the Magisterium.

Our mission is to evangelize, to spread the Crusade Prayers, to promote the Divine Will Spirituality of Luisa Piccarreta, and to help souls prepare for the Warning (Illumination of Conscience), the Great Miracle, and the coming of the New Era — the fulfillment of the Our Father: "Thy Kingdom come, Thy Will be done, on earth as it is in Heaven."

"The Great Monarch will come when the world is in its darkest hour. He will restore all things in Christ and bring about the Eucharistic Reign of the Sacred Heart. The triumph of the Immaculate Heart of Mary will usher in an era of peace such as the world has never known."

— Catholic Prophecy on the Great Monarch

☩ Prayer of Allegiance to the Sacred Heart

O Sacred Heart of Jesus, I pledge my allegiance to You.
I give You my body, my soul, my will, and my entire life.
I consecrate to You all that I am and all that I have.
Lead me, guide me, protect me, and use me
as Your instrument of peace and salvation.
I reject Satan and all his works and all his empty promises.
I belong to You, Jesus, now and forever.
Amen.

🌅 The Most Powerful Morning Offering

O Immaculate Heart of Mary, I offer you this day
all my prayers, works, joys, and sufferings,
in union with the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass throughout the world,
in reparation for my sins, for the intentions of all our associates,
for the reunion of Christendom, and in particular
for the intentions of the Holy Father.

I desire to fuse my entire being into the Divine Will,
that every breath, heartbeat, and thought
may be an act done in the Divine Will of God.
I call upon all Creation to praise the Lord with me.
Amen.

⚔️ Prayer of the Victim Soul — Victimhood of Love

O my Jesus, I offer myself to You as a victim of love,
a holocaust of reparation for the sins of the world.
Consume me with the fire of Your Divine Love.
Let me suffer with You, for You, and in You,
that souls may be converted and saved.
I accept all trials, sufferings, and crosses
that You permit in my life,
united with Your Passion and Death on the Cross.
Mary, my Mother, stand with me at the foot of the Cross.
Amen.
